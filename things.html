Fishing

  • Kerry has some of the most exciting fishing in the world! Click to see all we have to offer -here-
  • As well as some of the best shore and rock fishing there's nothing like going to Fenit Pier and having a great day out.
  • The Killarney lakes are a wonderful area to fish there are boats to hire and a public slip as long as your boat is cleaned.
  • There are many marinas to visit and hiring a charter boat is one of the best wasy to go deep sea fishing.
